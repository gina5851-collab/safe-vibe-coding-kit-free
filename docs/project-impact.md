# Project Impact

Safe Vibe Coding Kit is a free open-source guide for non-coders, creators, educators, and small business owners who want to use AI coding tools safely.

Many people now use Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub, and Vercel to build websites, landing pages, AI stores, content hubs, and simple business systems. However, non-coders often face risks such as accidental file deletion, unwanted deployment, exposed API keys, broken production sites, or unclear AI-generated changes.

This project helps reduce those risks by providing practical safety rules, checklists, prompt templates, and beginner-friendly workflows.

The project is especially useful for Korean creators and small business owners who are learning AI-assisted development and need simple, reusable, safety-first guidance.

## Who this helps

- Non-coders using AI coding tools for the first time
- Creators building websites, landing pages, and content hubs
- Small business owners creating simple AI-assisted business systems
- Educators teaching practical AI workflows
- Korean entrepreneurs who need beginner-friendly safety guides

## Why this matters

AI coding tools are powerful, but they can also make changes too quickly for beginners to review. This project encourages a safer workflow:

1. Inspect first
2. Plan before editing
3. Protect private data
4. Review changed files
5. Do not commit, push, or deploy without approval

The goal is to make AI-assisted development more accessible, understandable, and safe for people outside traditional software engineering.
